# Privacy Policy – GWSC (Garden of the White Sand Club)

**Effective Date:** May 21, 2026
**Last Updated:** May 21, 2026

GWSC (Garden of the White Sand Club) ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the **GWSC mobile application**.

By using our application, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

When you use the application, we may collect:

* Full name
* Phone number
* Email address
* Password (securely encrypted)
* Profile photo
* Residential address
* Date of birth
* Membership application details
* Employment and qualification information
* Dependent/family member information
* Support inquiries and communication records

---

### Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

* Device model and operating system
* Device identifiers
* App usage statistics and interaction logs
* Crash reports and diagnostics
* IP address
* Push notification tokens
* App preferences and settings

---

### Location Information

With your permission, we may collect your device location to:

* Display club location and facilities
* Improve location-based services

You may disable location access at any time through your device settings.

---

### Payment Information

Payments within the app are securely processed by **Fawry Payment Services**.

GWSC does **not store or access your credit/debit card details**.

Please review Fawry’s Privacy Policy here:
https://fawry.com/privacy

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

* Create and manage your membership account
* Process membership applications and renewals
* Process payments and generate receipts
* Provide customer support
* Send notifications regarding club activities, events, and announcements
* Improve app performance and user experience
* Detect fraud and ensure platform security
* Comply with legal obligations

---

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **not sell** your personal data.

We may share your information with:

### Service Providers

Third-party vendors who assist us with:

* Cloud hosting

* **Fawry Payment Gateway**

### Legal Authorities

When required by law or to protect our legal rights.

## 4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including:

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to:

## 6. Your Rights

You may request to:
